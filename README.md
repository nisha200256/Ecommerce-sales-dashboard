# 📊 E-Commerce Sales & Customer Insights Dashboard

## 🔍 Overview
This project analyzes e-commerce data to understand revenue trends, customer behavior, and product performance. The goal is to derive actionable insights to support business decision-making.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features
- KPI metrics: Total Revenue, Total Orders, Average Order Value (AOV)
- Revenue trend analysis over time
- Customer segmentation (One-time vs Repeat customers)
- Product performance (Top-selling products)
- Payment method distribution
- Interactive filters (Month, Payment Type)

---

## 🧠 Key Insights
- Revenue shows fluctuations, indicating possible seasonality
- Majority of customers are one-time buyers (low retention)
- Repeat customers contribute significantly to total revenue
- Sales are concentrated among a small number of top products

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- Introduce customer retention strategies (loyalty programs)
- Focus marketing on high-value and repeat customers
- Optimize inventory for top-performing products
- Use seasonal trends to plan campaigns

---

## 🛠 Tools Used
- Power BI (Dashboard & Visualization)
- Python (Pandas, NumPy for data analysis)
- Google Colab (Data processing)
